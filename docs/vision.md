# Vision

Boson exists to give developers direct, open access to AI coding operators from their own machines.

## Problem

Modern AI-assisted coding is often bundled behind closed products, recurring subscriptions, and vendor lock-in.

## Belief

Developers should own their workflow:

- Choose their own models and providers
- Control what data leaves their machine
- Extend and audit their tools in the open

## Principles

- Local-first by default
- Open interfaces, not hardcoded providers
- Transparent architecture and behavior
- Security-conscious operator execution
- Community-driven evolution

## Outcome

Boson aims to be a trustworthy, open-source workbench where AI operator capabilities are powerful, inspectable, and user-controlled.
